Homemade Corn Tortillas

Description

Corn tortillas are a delicious and necessary part of any taco, but those that are homemade are irreplaceable. The distint taste and texture are only possible using the following method.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups masa harina
  • 3 cups hot water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dough and water
  2. Rest the dough for 10min
  3. Portion the dough into 2tbsp balls
  4. Press the dough balls into 5 inch tortillas
  5. Cook the tortillas for 45 seconds per side until golden brown
