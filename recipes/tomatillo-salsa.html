Roasted Tomatillo Salsa

Description

Roasted tomatillo salsa is the best part of a taco. It has a sharp citrus acidity, a constant freshness, and a tinge of natural sweetness. This salsa could just as easily be eaten with a spoon as it could with a tortilla chip or on a taco.

Ingredients

  • 5 tomatillos
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 bunch of cilantro

Steps

  1. Roast the tomatillos and jalapeno under a broiler until well browned
    2. =
  2. Blend everything together
  3. Enjoy
