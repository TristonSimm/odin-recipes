Eggplant Parmigiana

Description

The eggplant parmigiana is a delectible layered meal consisting of baked eggplant, pasta sauce, and cheeses. This recipe is simple enough for the most simple people. You will cut the eggplant into slices, bread it, and bake it until breading is browned. Then to a pan add a layers of sauce, eggplant, parmigiana, and mozzerella.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplant
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups pasta sauce
  • Italian breadcrumbs
  • 4 cups parmigiana
  • 4 cups mozzerella

Steps

  1. Cut eggplant into 1 inch slices
  2. Toss slices in egg wash and cover in bread crumbs
  3. Bake at 450f until breading is browned
  4. Put thin layer of sauce in bottom of pan
  5. Proceed to fill pan witih layers of eggplant, sauce, and the cheeses
  6. Bake until cheese is browned
